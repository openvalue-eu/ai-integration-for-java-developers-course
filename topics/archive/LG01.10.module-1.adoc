=== Learning Objectives
* Trace the historical shift from automation → augmentation → intelligent collaboration.
* Recognize how AI changes the developer’s role in modern software teams.
* Identify the main categories of AI-assisted tools available today.
* Appreciate both the productivity gains and limitations of AI co-development.
